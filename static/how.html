How to use YTGrab

One place for video and audio: fetch formats, pick a row, and download.

Quick start

  1. Go to Home, paste a YouTube URL, and hit Fetch formats.
  2. Review the preview card; use the tabs to switch between Audio and Video.
  3. Click a Download button in the table row you want. That’s it.

Audio only

  1. Stay on the Audio tab.
  2. Pick MP3 320 kbps (highest) or MP3 128 kbps (smaller size).
  3. Click Download to get an MP3 file.

Video

  1. Switch to the Video tab.
  2. Choose a resolution row (e.g., 1080p) and container (MP4/MKV) from the table.
  3. Click Download to grab the merged video + audio.

Tips

  • Use the filename box on Home to set a custom name before downloading.
  • If a download fails, ensure FFmpeg is installed and the YouTube link is public.
  • Temporary download links and history entries auto-expire after ~30 minutes.
