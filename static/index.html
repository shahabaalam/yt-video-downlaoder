YTGrab

Paste a YouTube link, fetch real qualities, choose MP4 or MKV, and download.

How to use YTGrab

  1. Paste any YouTube URL above and click “Fetch qualities”.
  2. Select one of the real qualities yt-dlp finds and choose MP4 or MKV.
  3. Optionally set a filename, then Download or copy a 30-minute link.
